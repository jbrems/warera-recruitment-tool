# Levels

In Warera there are 3 ways to gain experience:
- Starter missions
- Weekly missions
- Daily missions

## Starter missions
At the start of your Warera journey every player gets a set of 10 missions.
This is a one time set of missions that cannot be repeated.
Each mission gives you 50xp.
Upon completing 7 of the 10 missions you get a bonus of 500xp, 50 gold, and 5 cases.

## Weekly missions
Every week a player gets a new set of 10 missions to complete.
These missions reset every week on Monday at 0:00 GMT.
Each mission gives 30xp.
Upon completing 7 of the 10 missions you get a bonus of 300xp, 30 gold, and 3 cases.

## Daily missions
Every day a player gets a new set of 10 missions to complete.
These missions reset every day at 0:00 GMT.
Each mission gives 10xp.
Upon completing 7 of the 10 missions you get a bonus of 100xp, 10 gold, and 1 case.

## XP per level
A player with 0 xp will be level 1
A player with 110 xp will be level 2
A player with 200 xp will be level 3
A player with 1.32k xp will be level 7
A player with 1.62k xp will be level 9
A player with 5.27k xp will be level 14
A player with 6.77k xp will be level 15
A player with 7.80k xp will be level 16
A player with 11.93k xp will be level 19
A player with 13.41k xp will be level 20
A player with 15.50k xp will be level 21
A player with 16.65k xp will be level 22
A player with 23.57k xp will be level 25
A player with 34.26k xp will be level 31
A player with 68.6K xp will be level 44
Going from level 25 to level 26 requires an additional 2000xp.
Going from level 40 to level 41 requires an additional 3000xp.
